About Forces of Beauty®

The Forces of Beauty® 2022 report is a study sourced from approximately 4,000 women of various racial and ethnic backgrounds. In addition to valuable data, their voices lend firsthand narratives to the larger conversation around representation in beauty marketing.

Our research provides aesthetics practitioners, media, and industry leaders insights to better understand the dynamic between beauty and racial identity for women within the United States. Let's make the world of aesthetics more inclusive and better serve all patients.
