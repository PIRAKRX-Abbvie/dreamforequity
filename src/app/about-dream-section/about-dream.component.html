About the DREAM Initiative®

DREAM: Driving Racial Equity in Aesthetic Medicine® is a comprehensive initiative created by Allergan Aesthetics and skinbetter science®, designed to address effects of systemic racism in aesthetic medicine. The Forces of Beauty® 2022 report is the latest component of our program, which aims to further EED&I education within the world of aesthetics and the beauty industry as a whole.
